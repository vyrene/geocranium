Meet Evan

Evan Alderete — known online as Geocranium — is an American composer, musician, and sound designer whose music sits at the intersection of cinematic orchestration and classic video-game soundtracks.

His creative partnership with animator Gooseworx stretches back to high school, and it's that collaboration that produced two of the most talked-about scores in independent animation: the pilot of Hazbin Hotel, and The Amazing Digital Circus for Glitch Productions.

When the Digital Circus pilot exploded — becoming one of the most-watched animated pilots in YouTube history — Evan's music went with it. The official soundtrack landed on Spotify and every major streaming platform, spun off physical vinyl editions, and introduced his sound to a massive global audience of fans who stream, cover, and remix his cues daily.

A graduate of the DigiPen Institute of Technology, Evan pairs deep technical craft with a distinctive musical voice: playful, precise, and unafraid to get a little existential. He also works as an engineer at BigBoxVR, bringing the same rigor to interactive audio that he brings to the concert of chaos under the big top.
